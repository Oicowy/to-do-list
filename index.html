Technology List

OSIGHT TECHNOLOGY LINEUP

MCC™

Magnetic Charging Cover

Check remaining battery and charge the optic through a weatherproof magnetic interface without removing the sight from the setup.

  • Integrated LED battery display
  • Magnetic charging contact
  • Weather-resistant cover design
Battery Display

CBRS™

Collapsible Backup Rear Sight

A compact backup sight folds into the optic body and deploys quickly when the primary optic view is compromised.

  • Instant mechanical backup
  • Low-profile protected form
  • Built for emergency alignment
Deployable Sight

AMRS™

Advanced Multi-Reticle System

Switch between reticle modes to match the distance, lighting, and engagement style without losing visual clarity.

  • Multiple reticle combinations
  • Fast mode recognition
  • Adaptable sight picture
Reticle Modes

MAT™

Motion Activation Technology

The optic detects movement, wakes automatically, and conserves battery while remaining ready for immediate use.

  • Motion-triggered wake
  • Battery-saving standby
  • Always-ready optic status
Motion Wake
